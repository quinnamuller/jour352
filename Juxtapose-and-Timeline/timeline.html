Timeline

This timeline outlines Washington Capitals captain Alexander Ovechkin's GR8 Chase to become the NHL's all-time leading goal scorer. On this journey, Ovechkin surpassed hockey legends like Mark Messier, Jaromir Jagr, and lastly, Wayne Gretzky. Ovechkin has yet to retire and, even now, is still adding more goals to his record. He was drafter first overall in 2004 to the Capitals and he has been there ever since, breaking record after record to become a household name and one of the greatest hockey players of all time.
